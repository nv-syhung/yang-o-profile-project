Our story

Our meaning

Yang O Farm is our first ever farm located in district Mang Yang of Gia Lai province. In our native language, "Yang" means "sky". The "sky" blessed this area with a fertile ground, good climate so that agricultural activities can thrive and become well known. As a consequence, this became the primary source of income for the people here.

Our purpose

The agricultural landscape still poses risks to its workers. Therefore, Yang O decided to become a supporter for the hard working farmers here and contribute to the growth of the Vietnam agriculture economy, as well as commit ourselves to bringing these native products of the highest quality from rural farms into the wide domestic and foreign markets, and as a result make them become an integral part of the global cuisine.
