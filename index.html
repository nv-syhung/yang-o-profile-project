Our story

Yang O Farm is our first ever farm in district Mang Yang of Gia Lai province. In our native language, "Yang" means "sky". The "sky" blessed this area with a fertile ground, good climate so that agricultural activities can thrive and become well known. As a consequence, this became the primary source of income for the people here.

The agricultural landscape still have its risks. We, Yang O, decided to become a supporter for the hard working farmers here and contribute to the growth of the agricultural landscape of Vietnam, as well as commit ourselves to bringing these Vietnamese products of the highest quality from farms into domestic and foreign markets, and as a result make them become a part of the global cuisine.
